The Story Behind Our Game

Discover how "The Hunt For Knowledge" transforms learning into an epic time-traveling adventure.

Education Meets Adventure

Created by a team of passionate educators and game developers, "The Hunt For Knowledge" reimagines traditional learning as a third-person adventure game that transports students through different time periods.

Players must work together in pairs, answer curriculum-aligned questions, and defeat knowledge-based bosses to progress. One student controls movement (A and D keys) while the other handles actions (SPACE and LEFT MOUSE), creating a natural teamwork environment.

Our innovative 3D-printed wand controllers immerse students in the experience, making them feel like real wizards of knowledge as they journey through history, science, and more!

How The Game Works

Experience our unique educational gameplay mechanics designed for learning and collaboration.

Time Travel Adventure

Players explore immersive 3D worlds set in different historical periods and scientific contexts. From ancient Egypt to the far reaches of space, every environment is filled with educational content and challenges.

The third-person perspective allows students to see their characters navigate these rich environments, making history and science come alive in ways a textbook never could.

Two-Player Collaboration

Our innovative control system splits responsibilities between two students:

  • Player 1 controls movement (A and D keys)
  • Player 2 controls actions (SPACE and LEFT MOUSE)

This design naturally encourages verbal communication, planning, and teamwork. Students must coordinate their actions to navigate obstacles and solve puzzles, developing vital collaboration skills.

Question-Based Mechanics

To perform special actions in the game, students must correctly answer curriculum-aligned questions. These questions can be customized by teachers to match exactly what's being taught in class.

Correct answers unlock abilities like jumping over obstacles, opening doors, or activating special powers - turning knowledge into in-game progress.

Boss Battles

At key moments, the game transitions to exciting first-person boss battles inspired by classic RPGs. These knowledge showdowns test what students have learned throughout their adventure.

During boss battles, players must answer increasingly challenging questions to defeat historical figures or scientific concepts brought to life as game characters. The turn-based system gives students time to think and discuss before answering.

Interactive Learning Tools

Our specially designed 3D-printed wands transform the gaming experience by giving students tangible controllers that make them feel like they're casting spells of knowledge.

Each wand connects to the game system and translates physical movements into in-game actions. The movement wand controls character direction, while the action wand triggers jumps, interactions, and spell casting.

Schools can print these wands using standard 3D printers following our open-source designs, making implementation affordable and accessible for classrooms everywhere.
